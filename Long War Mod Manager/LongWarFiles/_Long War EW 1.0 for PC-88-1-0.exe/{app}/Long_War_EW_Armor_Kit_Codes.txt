Armor deco:
Possible armor settings run from 1 to 177 (with many holes that don't work), but for rookies you'll want to use kevlar decos only (note the final appearance will depend on whether you are using a light or heavy armor variant), which are 67 through 79. Note: 67 seems buggy, use -1 for that look. The decos assigned to these numbers *may* vary based on the DLCs you have, so you may need some trial and error to get the setting you want.
